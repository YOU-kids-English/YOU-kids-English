What We Teach

We offer two courses tailored to your child's level.

🌱
Beginners Course
¥7,500 / month　Once a week · 90 min

🎯 For: First-time learners or those revisiting the basics

What you'll learn

  • Start gently with the alphabet and phonics
  • Build vocabulary and key phrases through Aesop's Fables
  • Practice basic daily conversation with our native teacher
  • Fun activities using pictures and movement
  • Balanced practice of listening, speaking, and reading
🏁 GoalHold everyday conversations with confidence
🚀
Advanced Course
¥12,000 / month　Once a week · 120 min

🎯 For: Students with basic English who want to level up

What you'll learn

  • In-depth reading & discussion using Aesop's Fables
  • Speech practice to express opinions in English
  • Free-talk & discussion with our native teacher
  • Introduction to English essay writing
  • Support for EIKEN and school exams
